‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes for Beginners

‚úÖ Image Filename Rules:
  • No spaces in filenames (use hyphens or underscores)
  • Good: my-window.jpg, product_1.png
  • Bad: my window.jpg, product 1.png
üìÅ Where to Upload Images:
  • Go to the images folder in your repository
  • Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"
  • Drag and drop your images or click to browse
  • Click "Commit changes" to save
üîÑ How to Replace Placeholder Images:
  • Find lines like: <img src="images/sample.jpg" ... />
  • Replace sample.jpg with your actual filename
  • Keep the images/ part before the filename
  • Example: Change to <img src="images/my-photo.jpg" ... />
üì∏ Supported Image Formats:
  • JPG/JPEG (best for photos)
  • PNG (best for logos with transparency)
  • GIF (for animated images)
  • SVG (for scalable graphics)
